A) Equipment for Messa di Voce DVD Viewing Area:

  • 1 PAL DVD player
  • 1 Plasma screen, LCD monitor, or large CRT television
  • small stereo sound system (powered speakers)
  • video & audio cables for above
  • 3 or 4 soft chairs or stools

B) Video Equipment for Interactive Installation:

  • 1 LCD computer monitor, XVGA (1024x768)
  • 1 XVGA video projector, at least 2000 Lumens
  • 1 fixturing or mounting system for video projector
  • 1 VGA cable, 15 meters (used for projector)
  • 1 coaxial video cable, 15 meters, 75-ohm with BNC-connectors

C) Audio Equipment for Interactive Installation:

  • 2 dynamic cardioid microphones, ideally Shure SM57
  • 2 XLR balanced audio cables, 15 meters
  • 2 standard adjustable microphone stands
  • 1 small submixer with XLR, for example Behringer Eurorack UB1002

D) 220-Volt Power Equipment for Interactive Installation:

  • 4 Euro-220v (Schoko) multiplier strips with 6 outlets each
  • 1 Euro-220v small mini-multiplier with 2 outlets
  • 1 Euro-220v power extension cable, 15 meters (for projector)
  • 4 power adapters: 220vAC-to-12vDC, 1000mA
    Note: these power adapters should have multi-connectors

E) Required Construction & Labor:

  • "Stage" platform with wheelchair ramp (300x225x20cm)
  • Computer Closet, approximately 100 wide x 300 deep, up to ceiling
    Note: Computer closet must have line-of-sight to interaction area!
  • Mounting of video projector from ceiling
  • White paint or canvas for video projection area (300x225cm, 50cm from floor)
  • Black paint for walls, closet exterior, and stage platform
  • White tape on edge of stage platform
