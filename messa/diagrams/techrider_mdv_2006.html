Equipment requested for the INSTALLATION:

  • Messa di Voce

COMPUTER EQUIPMENT:

  • 1 (one) PC tower computer, Intel Pentium 3.0Ghz+, WindowsXP (English OS please!), 512+ MB RAM.
    Recommended manufacturers are Dell or HP.
  • 1 (one) nVidia GeForce graphics card, dual-head, 6000-series or 7000-series model.
  • 1 (one) wireless English keyboard & wireless mouse, with at least 10-foot wireless range.
  • 1 (one ) small table & 2 (two) chairs, for working/preparation.

VIDEO EQUIPMENT:

  • 1 (one) LCD screen, 1024x768 resolution, 15" preferably, with VESA wall-mount.
    One additional LCD screen could be very helpful during setup.
  • 1 (one) video data projector, XGA 1024x768, 3000+ lumens, with DLP technology (not LCD).
  • 1 (one) fixturing system or mounting bracket for the video projector
  • 1 (one) VGA cable, 20 meters or as long as necessary.
  • 1 (one) coaxial video cable, 20 meters, 75-ohm with BNC-connectors

AUDIO EQUIPMENT:

  • 2 (two) dynamic cardioid microphones, ideally Shure SM57
  • 2 (two) XLR balanced microphone audio cables, 15 meters
  • 2 (two) standard adjustable microphone boom stands
  • 1 (one) small submixer with XLR inputs, for example Behringer Eurorack UB1002

DVD PLAYING EQUIPMENT:

  • 1 (one) DVD player (please specify PAL or NTSC!)
  • 1 (one) plasma screen for use with a DVD player
  • 1 pair of powered audio speakers for use with DVD player,
    or an audio amplifier with a pair of speakers.
  • video/audio cables to connect DVD with plasma and speakers
  • 4 (four) large pillows or bean-bag chairs

POWER EQUIPMENT:

  • 6 (six) 100V (Japan) or 120V (USA) or 240V (Euro), 6-outlet power strips.
  • 1 (one) power extension cable for video projector
  • 5 (five) DC power adaptors: 12VDC, 1000mA, ideally with selectable connectors

REQUIRED CONSTRUCTION & LABOR:

  • "Stage" platform with wheelchair ramp (330x225x20cm)
  • Computer room, at least 200 wide x 300 deep, up to ceiling
    Note: Computer room must have line-of-sight to interaction area! If possible, we prefer
    not to have a computer closet, but instead locate the PC behind the wall to an adjacent room.
    (We feel the computer closet looks clunky if it is a standalone construction.
    Please contact us about the possible solutions for your venue.)
  • Mounting of video projector from ceiling
  • White paint or canvas for video projection area (300x225cm, 50cm from floor)
  • Black paint for walls, closet exterior, and stage platform
  • White (or better, glow-in-the-dark) tape on edge of stage platform
  • One long piece of wood, approximately 250x10x6. This will be used for mounting IR lights.
